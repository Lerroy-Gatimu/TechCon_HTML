Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small local meetup into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Over the years, the conference has served as a launchpad for innovations, collaborations, and global connections.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to inspiring innovation, fostering collaboration, and shaping the future of technology. Our mission is to provide a platform where ideas transform into impactful solutions that address global challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has proudly hosted some of the brightest minds in the industry. Here are just a few of our past keynote speakers:

Dr. Ada Johnson

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Johnson has contributed groundbreaking research in machine learning and its ethical applications. She inspired countless innovators at TechCon 2018.

Michael Lee

As a cybersecurity leader, Michael Lee has developed global strategies for safeguarding data. His keynote at TechCon 2020 emphasized resilience in the digital age.

Sophia Martinez

Founder of a leading green-tech startup, Sophia Martinez spoke at TechCon 2022 about sustainable technology and the responsibility of innovators in addressing climate change.
